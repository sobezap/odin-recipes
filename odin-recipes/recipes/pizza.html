Pizza Recipe

As you can see, here is our beautiful pizza recipe that will easily please your friends and family!

Ingredients

  • Fresh dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Mozzerella and Gorgonzola Cheese
  • Patience
  1. Grab dough, carefully spreading it thinner and thinner
  2. Spread sauce generously
  3. layer cheese evenly, applying salt and pepper after
  4. Place in oven at 500 Degrees F for 10 minutes
