Lasagna Recipe

As you can see, here is our beautiful lasagna recipe that will easily please your friends and family!

Ingredients

  • Dried Lasaga
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Mozerella and Ricotta Cheese
  • Patience
  1. First grab pan and grease
  2. Place dry lasagna sheets in the bottom
  3. layer cheese and sauce evenly
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 until either out of supplies or out of space
  5. Place in oven at 450 Degrees F for 45 minutes
