Peanut Butter and Jelly Recipe

As you can see, here is our beautiful Peanut Butter and Jelly Recipe that will easily please your friends and family!

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Butter knife
  • Patience
  1. First lay out two slices of bread
  2. Using butter knife, spread peanut butter across one slice of bread
  3. Repeat step 2 on the other slice with jelly
  4. Put two slices together, cutting down the middle to form two pieces
  5. Enjoy!
